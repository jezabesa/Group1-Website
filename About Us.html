Launched in 2021, Ali Ritzy is Philippines' leading omnichannel platform with a national
mindset: on trend, online, on-the-go. Ali Ritzy started its humble beginnings in Social
media platforms especially in Facebook as an online thrift shop. But as we expand, we
want to give something that is not limited and that everyone can have.

We started
selling both thrifted and brand new clothes. Our thrifted clothes are catering buyers who
want to have a rare and one of a kind cloth that he/she can wear without worrying to
have the same exact cloth as others. On the other hands, our brand new clothes are
catering to buyers who want to have the clothes they want, regardless of the fact
that others might have the same purchased cloth as they have. Offering quality yet
affordable clothes which deserve to be part in everyone's wardrobe. Ali Ritzy is
shipping your orders nationwide, TEMPORARILY, as we wanted to expand our shop globally.
